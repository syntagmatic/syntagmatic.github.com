Notes

Unemployed: U-3 Total unemployed, as a percent of the civilian labor force (official unemployment rate)
Discouraged: U-5 Total unemployed, plus discouraged workers, plus all other persons marginally attached to the labor force, as a percent of the civilian labor force plus all persons marginally attached to the labor force
Part-time: U-6 Total unemployed, plus all persons marginally attached to the labor force, plus total employed part time for economic reasons, as a percent of the civilian labor force plus all persons marginally attached to the labor force

Source: Bureau of Labor Statistics
